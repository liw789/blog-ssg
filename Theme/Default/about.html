About

Frame x Frame is a simple photo blog I created to combine my two hobbies, programming and photography. On the programming side, this site is generated from a python program I wrote that takes a list of photos and generates the HTML and CSS for the site. On the photography side, I'm always looking for new ways to improve my photography skills and I hope you enjoy the photos I share here. If you have any questions or comments, feel free to reach out to me on Instagram or GitHub. Thanks for visiting!
